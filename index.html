What FECI is about

The Integrative Clinical Assessment Form (FECI) has been used developed in the context of EIS Integrative Psychotherapy as a measure to assess Personality, Self-esteem, Self-image, perceived Self-efficacy, information processing styles, Self-control, Symptoms, and behavioral management. It comprises internationally validated scales like Eysenck's EPQ, Rathus Assertiveness Schedule, The Gambrill & Ritchie Assertion Inventory Sub-scale, and a series of scales developed at ICPSI.

FECI-Measurements is a website designed for clinicians to collect, score, and interpret data about their patients. FECI-analyst allows you to keep track of your patient's progress, and get useful hints to improve psychotherapy outcomes.
